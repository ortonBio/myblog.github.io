To create any item in the game, you'll need reagents. The quality of crafted items depends on your profession skill and the quality of materials you use â€” the higher your skill, the higher the final quality.

> *The higher your **SKILL**, the less **Concentration** you need to spend to craft high-quality items. A higher skill level also allows you to use lower-rank reagents to achieve high-quality results.*

In the latest expansions, professions are also influenced by factors such as:

- Chosen specialization (except Cooking, Fishing and Archaeology)
- Quality of tools and accessories used

First things first, let's talk about your tools. In *Midnight*, they are more critical than ever. It's not just about leveling upâ€”high-quality tools are essential for saving gold and maximizing your long-term earnings.
