## PROFESSION OVERVIEW

To create any item in the game, you'll need reagents. The quality of crafted items depends on your profession skill and the quality of materials you use ‚Äî the higher your skill, the higher the final quality.

> *The higher your **SKILL**, the less **Concentration** you need to spend to craft high-quality items. A higher skill level also allows you to use lower-rank reagents to achieve high-quality results.*

In the latest expansions, professions are also influenced by factors such as:

- Chosen specialization (except Cooking, Fishing and Archaeology)
- Quality of tools and accessories used

Understanding these rules is one thing, but navigating the *most efficient path* to max skill is another. Wasting reagents on the wrong recipes is a classic gold sink‚Äîwhich is exactly what we'll avoid next. 

Let's map out your optimal leveling guide.

### SKILL 1-100

#### 1-25 (Eversong Woods)

Skin beasts in Eversong Woods until skill 25 to unlock Specializations. Then, choose [Talented Tracker](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/talented-tracker-106119) and put 40 Knowledge Points into it to be able to kill Renowned Beasts for rare reagents such as:

- [Majestic Claw](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=238528/majestic-claw)
- [Majestic Hide](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=238529/majestic-hide)
- [Majestic Fin](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=238530/majestic-fin)


> üí° **Note:** You can earn 24 bonus Knowledge Points early on by collecting 8 Skinning Treasures in each Midnight Zone.

#### 25-50 (Zul'Aman)

Skin beasts in Zul'Aman until you reach skill 50 to unlock your second Specialization. Then, choose [Gainful Gathering](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/gainful-gathering-106059) and allocate 10 Knowledge Points into it to unlock the sub-specialization, then select [Dedicated Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/dedicated-diffuser-106056).

**Why?** By allocating 40 KPs into [Dedicated Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/dedicated-diffuser-106056), this will unlock 4 Diffusing Devices that can be used to receive additional motes when skinning:

- [Lightbloom Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225939/lightbloom-diffuser) for more [Mote of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236949/mote-of-light)
- [Wild Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225941/wild-diffuser) for more [Mote of Wild Magic](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236951/mote-of-wild-magic)
- [Primal Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225940/primal-diffusers) for more [Mote of Primal Energy](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236950/mote-of-primal-energy)
- [Void Diffuser](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225942/void-diffuser) for more [Mote of Pure Void](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236952/mote-of-pure-void)



#### 50-75 (Harandar)

Skin beasts in Harandar until you reach skill 75 to unlock your third Specialization ([Thorough Tanning](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/thorough-tanning-106089)).

**Why?** By allocating 40 KPs into [Thorough Tanning](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/thorough-tanning-106089), this will unlock [Sharpen Your Knife](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1223388/sharpen-your-knife) that can be used to receive a hide or scale from your next Midnight skinning.

#### 75-100 (Voidstorm)

From levels 75 to 100, explore Voidstorm to reach the level cap and become familiar with all Midnight zones. You can then choose your favorite one to farm.
