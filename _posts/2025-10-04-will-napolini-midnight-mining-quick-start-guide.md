- Chosen specialization (except Cooking, Fishing, and Archaeology)
- Quality of tools and accessories used

## ORES OF MIDNIGHT

### Base Ores Overview

Midnight introduces three core ores and their Empowered variations:

#### The Three Base Ores

1. [Refulgent Copper Ore](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=237359)

### Empowered Ore Variations

There are three different types of empowered ores you will come across while leveling Mining. These special nodes provide additional benefits beyond standard gathering.

#### 1. Lightfused Ores

A strange light has begun to afflict the ores growing within Quel'thalas. They grow large and golden, reminiscent of the Sunwell itself, but twisted somehow. Gathering these ores yields a searing explosion of holy energy that harms the miner unless they properly learn how to handle the outpouring light.

**Affected Ores:**
- [Lightfused Brilliant Silver](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225359/lightfused-brilliant-silver)
- [Lightfused Refulgent Copper](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225351/lightfused-refulgent-copper)
- [Lightfused Umbral Tin](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225367/lightfused-umbral-tin)

**Drops:** [Mote of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236949)

**Location:** Can be found in [Eversong Woods](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/zone=15968/eversong-woods)

#### 2. Wild Ores

Gathering these wild ores releases a burst of nature magic, giving rise to a group of hostile ore lashers that attempt to take vengeance!

**Affected Ores:**
- [Wild Brilliant Silver](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225363/wild-brilliant-silver)
- [Wild Refulgent Copper](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225353/wild-refulgent-copper)
- [Wild Umbral Tin](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225368/wild-umbral-tin)

**Drops:** [Mote of Wild Magic](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236951)

**Location:** Can be found in [Zul'Aman](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/zone=15947/zulaman)

#### 3. Primal Ores

Upon gathering these primal ores, they gift you with some of their energy in the form of additional Vigor for your Skyriding mount.

**Affected Ores:**
- [Primal Brilliant Silver](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225361/primal-brilliant-silver)
- [Primal Refulgent Copper](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225354/primal-refulgent-copper)
- [Primal Umbral Tin](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225369/primal-umbral-tin)

**Drops:** [Mote of Primal Energy](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236950)

**Location:** Can be found in [Harandar](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/zone=15355/harandar)

#### 4. Voidbound Ores

While the power of the void has been present on Azeroth since time immemorial, recent events have caused it to surge. It has managed to seep into the world, which has had adverse effects on the local ecology. In some cases, the unstable energies of the void have caused these ores to be sent across the Twisting Nether to parts unknown.

**Affected Ores:**
- [Voidbound Brilliant Silver](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225362/voidbound-brilliant-silver)
- [Voidbound Refulgent Copper](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225352/voidbound-refulgent-copper)
- [Voidbound Umbral Tin](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1225370/voidbound-umbral-tin)

**Drops:** [Mote of Pure Void](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=236952)

**Location:** Can be found in [Voidstorm](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/zone=15458/voidstorm)

### SKILL 1-100

#### 1-25 (Eversong Woods)

Farm ore in Eversong Woods until skill 25 to unlock Specializations. Then, choose [Meticulous Mining](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/meticulous-mining-105475) and put 40 Knowledge Points into it to gather while mounted.

> ğŸ’¡ **Note:** You can earn 24 bonus Knowledge Points early on by collecting 8 Mining Treasures in each Midnight Zone.

#### 25-50 (Zul'Aman)

Farm ore in Zul'Aman until you reach skill 50 to unlock your second Specialization. Then, choose [Plentiful Ores](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/plentiful-ores-105568) and allocate 5 Knowledge Points into it to unlock the sub-specialization, then select [Refulgent Copper](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/profession-trait/refulgent-copper-105567).

**Why?** This will allow you to gather more [Refulgent Copper Ore](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=237359), which is heavily used for [Gleaming Copper Ring](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1230489/gleaming-copper-ring) (Disenchanting) and [Refulgent Copper Ingot](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1230761/refulgent-copper-ingot). This high demand makes [Refulgent Copper Ore](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=237359) more valuable than any other ore in the Midnight expansion.

> ğŸ’¡ **Note:** All ores are valuable, but some are more valuable than others. Prices depend on supply and demand; if the market is flooded with an ore, its price will drop. Generally, the greater the scarcity, the higher the price.

#### 50-75 (Harandar)

Farm ore in Harandar until you reach skill 75 to unlock your third Specialization.

However, do not spend any Knowledge Points until all other specializations are complete.

#### 75-100 (Voidstorm)

From levels 75 to 100, explore Voidstorm to reach the level cap and become familiar with all Midnight zones. You can then choose your favorite one to farm.
