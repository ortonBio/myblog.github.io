## Enchanting Tools

First things first, let's talk about your tools. In The War Within, they play a much bigger role than before. It's not just about leveling upâ€”your tools are key to saving gold and earning more in the long run.

A high-quality tool does two crucial things for an Enchanter:

1. It boosts your skill, which directly increases the quality of reagents you get from disenchanting gear.
2. It gives you a chance to save reagents every time you craft an enchant, meaning each precious material goes further.

Because of this, getting the best tools you can afford will be one of your main priorities. Think of it as a foundational investment that pays off every time you open your profession window.

## Leveling Khaz Algar Enchanting

### Racials
*   **Blood Elf**: +5 Enchanting skill
*   **Kul Tiran**: +2 Enchanting skill

More Enchanting skill means more orange recipe skill-ups, saving gold and improving high-quality reagent chances from disenchanting.

### Disenchanting
Disenchant any leftover gear from questing or dungeons before you start leveling. Disenchanting can give skill points up to 25.

### Below is my step-by-step leveling guide:

Our goal is to unlock [Shimmering Dust](https://www.wowhead.com/item=227662) for KP's catch-up and [Artisan's Acuity](https://www.wowhead.com/item=210814/artisans-acuity)

#### First Step:

Disenchant x10-15 [Pioneer's Cloth Cuffs](https://www.wowhead.com/item=222833) or any other TWW gear until you get x5 [Fleeting Arcane Manifestation](https://www.wowhead.com/item=227659) and x1 [Gleaming Telluric Crystal](https://www.wowhead.com/item=227661)

#### Step Two:

Depending on the week, there are 3 Enchanting quests that will be available from [Nagad](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=219085/nagad) when you hit 25 skill:

1. [A Rare Necessity](https://www.wowhead.com/quest=84086/a-rare-necessity) - Requires x1 [Refulgent Crystal](https://www.wowhead.com/item=219952/refulgent-crystal)

2. [Just a Pinch](https://www.wowhead.com/quest=84084/just-a-pinch) - Requires x20 [Storm Dust](https://www.wowhead.com/item=219946/storm-dust)

3. [The Power of Potential](https://www.wowhead.com/quest=84085/the-power-of-potential) - Requires x10 [Gleaming Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/item=219949/gleaming-shard)

#### Step Three:

When you complete the quest, head out of Dornogal and loot [Crystalline Repository](https://www.wowhead.com/item=225230) and [Powdered Fulgurance](https://www.wowhead.com/item=225231) from [Earthbound Geode](https://www.wowhead.com/object=444091). When you do this, [Shimmering Dust](https://www.wowhead.com/item=227662) will be unlocked and you're ready to go further.

![Earthbound Geode Map](https://res.cloudinary.com/dlkv9y3s2/image/upload/v1759959884/will-napolini-enchanting-quick-start-guide-map-earthbound-geode_tzsgze.png)

> **Pro Tip:** [Myu's Weak Aura](https://wago.io/L7lpDrqUO) for tracking Profession Knowledge. Fantastic for tracking available profession knowledge, especially catchup.

## 25 - 47 (Recommended Path)

By allocating **5 points** into the **Ephemerals, Enrichments, and Equipment** specialization and then **15 points** into the **[Deceptive Decorations](https://www.wowhead.com/profession-trait/99939)** sub-specialization, you'll unlock **22 new recipes**.

![Deceptive Decorations](https://res.cloudinary.com/dlkv9y3s2/image/upload/v1762291620/will-napolini-enchanting-quick-start-guide-skill-25-50_p5caid.png) 

These recipes are consumable items that transform you into another race, such as the [Gleeful Glamour - Worgen](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445397), which changes you into a Worgen. You can earn First Craft bonuses from all of these recipes, totaling 22 knowledge points for the 20 you invest, along with around 23 Enchanting skill points.
